Dive logging without compromise.

Submersion is a comprehensive dive log that works on all your devices. No mandatory accounts. No subscription fees. No vendor lock-in. Your data stays yours.

Designed for divers who want full control of their data.

Traditional dive logging solutions either rely on outdated desktop software or mobile applications that restrict data access behind mandatory accounts and proprietary cloud infrastructure. Submersion offers a fundamentally different approach.

Complete Data Ownership

Your dives stay on your device. Sync to the cloud only if you want to. Export your logbook whenever you want.

Vendor Independence

Full UDDF 3.2 import and export capability, plus CSV support. Your dive history remains portable—both now and for years to come.

Unified Cross-Platform Experience

Available for iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, and Linux—consistent logbook functionality and analytics across all devices.

Free, forever

Open-source software licensed under GPL‑3.0. No advertisements. No premium feature restrictions.

Comprehensive capabilities for recreational and technical diving.

Streamlined workflows for routine logging with advanced functionality available when required.

Dive Documentation

  • Complete dive records including conditions, timestamps, and temperatures
  • Trip organization with tags, favorites, and detailed notes
  • Multi-cylinder configurations with gas mix tracking (air, nitrox, trimix)

Site Management

  • GPS coordinate recording with interactive map visualization
  • Comprehensive site data: depth ranges, difficulty ratings, hazard documentation
  • Automatic location capture with reverse geocoding

Dive Computer Integration

  • Direct USB and Bluetooth connectivity
  • Incremental download with intelligent duplicate detection
  • Support for 300+ models via libdivecomputer

Technical Diving Support

  • Bühlmann ZH‑L16C decompression algorithm with gradient factor configuration
  • Real-time NDL, ceiling, TTS, CNS, and OTU calculations
  • Complete multi-gas dive planning and tracking
