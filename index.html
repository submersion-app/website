Built for divers who want control.

Most logbooks are either dated desktop apps or mobile apps that trap your data behind accounts and proprietary clouds. Submersion is different.

You control your data

Data is stored locally in SQLite. Sync is your choice. Export your full logbook to open standards at any time.

No lock‑in

UDDF 3.2 import/export + CSV. Your dive history stays portable — now and years from now.

One app everywhere

iOS, Android, macOS, Windows, and Linux — the same logbook and analytics on every device.

Free forever

Open source (GPL‑3.0). No ads. No premium tiers for core features.

Features that scale from rec to tech.
